📚 Academic Stress in Students

📌 Common Causes

  • Exams & performance pressure
  • Unrealistic expectations
  • Fear of failure
  • Time management issues
  • Heavy workload

🧠 Effects on Wellbeing

  • Anxiety, burnout
  • Sleep issues
  • Lack of motivation
  • Low confidence

🛠️ Coping Tips

  • Break study tasks into chunks
  • Use planners or Pomodoro timers
  • Practice deep breathing
  • Talk to friends, mentors

🔗 Helpful Resources

  • Khan Academy
  • Notion for Students
  • Pomofocus Timer
  • Quizlet (Study Tools)
  • Coursera (Free Courses)
  • StudySmarter
  • Calm (Meditation)

🎯 Practical Solutions

  • Time-blocking: Assign fixed hours to study, breaks, and revision using a digital planner.
  • Smart Notes: Use the Cornell method or mind maps to summarize lessons efficiently.
  • Peer Study Groups: Join small, focused groups for shared learning and accountability.
  • Mock Tests: Take weekly mock exams to build confidence and reduce fear of real exams.
  • Ask for Help: Email teachers or attend tutorials to clarify difficult topics early.
