⏰ Time Management

📌 Common Time Issues

  • Procrastination
  • Overcommitting
  • Distractions (phone, social media)
  • Poor planning
  • Last-minute cramming

🧠 Impact on Wellbeing

  • Stress and anxiety
  • Missed deadlines
  • Low productivity
  • Sleep deprivation

🛠️ Coping Strategies

  • Use planners or digital calendars
  • Set daily/weekly goals
  • Prioritize important tasks
  • Take regular breaks
  • Limit distractions

🔗 Helpful Resources

  • Todoist
  • Notion
  • Pomofocus Timer
  • Google Calendar
  • RescueTime (Productivity Tracker)
  • Habitica (Gamified Task Manager)

🎯 Time Management Tips

  • Pomodoro Technique: Work in focused sprints with short breaks.
  • Batch Tasks: Group similar tasks together to save time.
  • Set Boundaries: Learn to say no to non-essential commitments.
  • Reflect: Review your progress weekly and adjust plans.
  • Self-Care: Schedule downtime and rest.
