🧘‍♀️ Physical & Mental Health

🏋️ Common Physical Issues

  • Fatigue due to lack of sleep
  • Back pain from prolonged sitting
  • Skipping meals or poor diet
  • Inactivity/lack of exercise
  • Screen time fatigue

🧠 Common Mental Health Issues

  • Anxiety or panic attacks
  • Feeling overwhelmed or isolated
  • Burnout and exhaustion
  • Lack of motivation
  • Imposter syndrome

🛠️ Self-Care Tips

  • Establish a sleep schedule
  • Hydrate and eat balanced meals
  • Take short walks or stretch breaks
  • Meditate or journal daily
  • Unplug from screens at night

🔗 Helpful Resources

  • Headspace
  • MyFitnessPal
  • Mental Health Resources
  • Calm (Mindfulness & Sleep)
  • BetterHelp (Online Therapy)
  • British Nutrition Foundation
  • Sleep Foundation

💡 Healthy Habits

  • Routine: Stick to consistent wake/sleep times
  • Movement: Stretch every hour or walk 30 mins/day
  • Nutrition: Eat fruits, veggies, and whole grains
  • Rest: Allow time to relax without guilt
  • Support: Talk to friends or a counselor
